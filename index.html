Privacy Policy

Still Blocks — Last updated: March 2026

Still Blocks ("we", "our", "the app") is a mobile puzzle game. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data we collect

We do not collect any personal data. Still Blocks does not require registration, login, or any personal information to use. Your game progress, scores, streaks, and settings are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to our servers.

We do not have a server or database. We do not store any user data externally.

2. Advertising

The free version of Still Blocks displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect certain data for the purpose of serving personalized or non-personalized ads. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location data (country, region)
  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • App usage data (session duration, interactions with ads)

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us. You can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

If you purchase the "Remove Ads" option, no advertising data is collected as no ads are displayed.

3. In-app purchases

Still Blocks offers an optional in-app purchase to remove advertisements. These transactions are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not receive, process, or store any payment information.

4. Children's privacy

Still Blocks is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children. Since the app does not collect personal data from any user, no special measures for children's data are necessary.

5. Data storage and security

All game data (scores, streaks, settings) is stored locally on your device using standard device storage. This data is not encrypted beyond the device-level encryption provided by your operating system. If you delete the app, all local data is permanently deleted.

6. Your rights (GDPR / DSGVO)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access: Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access.
  • Right to deletion: You can delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the app.
  • Right to object to advertising: You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings (iOS: Settings — Privacy — Tracking; Android: Settings — Privacy — Ads).
  • Right to purchase ad-free: You can remove all advertising by purchasing the "Remove Ads" option within the app.

For any questions regarding advertising data collected by Google AdMob, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

7. Ad consent (EU / EEA)

For users in the European Economic Area, Still Blocks displays a consent dialog before showing personalized advertisements, in accordance with the GDPR and ePrivacy Directive. You may choose to see personalized ads, non-personalized ads, or purchase the ad-free version.

8. Third-party services

Still Blocks uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — Advertising. Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store — Distribution and in-app purchases. Subject to their respective privacy policies.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
